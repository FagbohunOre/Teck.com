How does smartlock works.?

Smart locks use various technologies, including Bluetooth, Wi-Fi, or Zigbee, to connect with other devices like smartphones, tablets, or smart home hubs. Users can control the lock remotely through a mobile app or via voice commands.

Can I grant temporal access.?

Yes, many smart locks allow you to create temporary or guest access codes for family members, friends, or service providers. Some also offer time-sensitive codes for added security.

Can I still use a physical key with a smart lock?.

Many smart locks retain the option for traditional keys as a backup. This can be useful in case of a dead battery or technical issues with the smart lock.
